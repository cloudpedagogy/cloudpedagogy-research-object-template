# Specification

This is the specification document outlining the structure, requirements, and principles of the Healthcare Research Object Template.

## Principles
1. **Reproducibility**: All data, code, and decisions should be documented.
2. **Governance**: Risks, ethical considerations, and model details must be transparent.
3. **Optional AI Support**: AI scaffolds are provided for reflection but are not required for functionality.

## Folder Structure
- `content/`: Core research narrative
- `analysis/`: Analytical plans and notes
- `data/`: Data dictionaries and codebooks
- `governance/`: Essential governance artefacts
- `ai-support/`: Prompts and reflection checkpoints
- `deploy/`: Publication instructions
