Welcome to the \textbf{Missed Appointment Study} Research Object.

This is a synthetic healthcare research project demonstrating the use of
the Healthcare Research Object Template.

\chapter{Study Overview}\label{study-overview}

This synthetic study investigates the factors associated with missed
outpatient appointments within a generic healthcare setting. The goal is
to identify predictors such as age, lead time, appointment type,
previous missed appointments, and socio-economic deprivation to better
understand service utilisation patterns.

Use the navigation bar to access: - \textbf{Content}: The core research
narrative (Question to Limitations). - \textbf{Analysis}: Our analysis
plan and ongoing notes. - \textbf{Data}: Our data dictionary describing
the synthetic variables. - \textbf{Governance}: Important transparent
documentation, including dataset and model cards. - \textbf{AI Support}:
Our reflections on the use of AI tools in this synthetic project.
