State the primary research question and objectives of the project.

\chapter{Details}\label{details}

\textbf{Primary Research Question}: What patient and system factors
predict the likelihood of a missed outpatient appointment in adult
ambulatory care settings?

\textbf{Study Aim}: To develop an exploratory logistic regression model
identifying the strength of association between historical missed
appointments, socio-demographic indicators (deprivation quintile),
appointment characteristics (lead time, appointment type), and the
outcome of a missed scheduled visit.

\textbf{Primary Outcome}: - \texttt{missed\_appointment}: Binary outcome
(0 = Attended or Cancelled appropriately, 1 = Did Not Attend/Missed).

\textbf{Key Predictors of Interest}: - \texttt{age}: Patient age in
years. - \texttt{lead\_time\_days}: Days between scheduling the
appointment and the appointment date. - \texttt{appointment\_type}: Type
of consultation (e.g., Initial, Follow-up, Routine). -
\texttt{previous\_missed}: Count of missed appointments in the previous
12 months. - \texttt{deprivation\_quintile}: Socio-economic status
indicator (1 = Most deprived, 5 = Least deprived).
