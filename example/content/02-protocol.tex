\chapter{Details}\label{details}

Overview of Study Design: This is a retrospective observational cohort
study utilizing a fully synthetic, cross-sectional snapshot of
ambulatory care data.

Inclusion Criteria: - Adult simulated patients (Age \(\ge\) 18). -
Scheduled for an outpatient visit between January 1, 2023, and December
31, 2023.

Exclusion Criteria: - Appointments cancelled with \textgreater24 hours
notice (these are not considered `missed' for the purpose of the primary
outcome).

Procedure: 1. Data Extraction: Synthetic generation of the cohort
dataset. 2. Data Cleaning: Removal of logical inconsistencies (e.g.,
negative lead times). 3. Exploratory Data Analysis: Assessing
distributions and collinearity among predictors. 4. Model Fitting:
Logistic regression.
