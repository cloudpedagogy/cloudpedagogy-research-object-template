# Prompt Pack: Ethics, Equity & Impact

Use these prompts to explore the broader implications of your research or AI tool usage.

## Prompt 1: Bias Identification
"I am designing a study using [Dataset Type] to predict [Outcome]. What are the historical or systemic biases commonly found in this type of data, and how might they impact my model's fairness?"

## Prompt 2: Impact Assessment
"Who are the stakeholders most likely to be negatively impacted by false positives or false negatives in a model predicting [Outcome]? Help me brainstorm potential harms and mitigation strategies."
